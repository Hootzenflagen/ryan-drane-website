Ryan Drane is a multi-talented comedian, director, and writer based in the United States. A self-described "super excited artist that writes, draws, and films as much as possible before that big death at the end," Ryan brings boundless enthusiasm and creativity to everything he creates.

As the creator of Down the Drane, Ryan has built an impressive portfolio with over 1,000 subscribers and nearly 600,000 views across 576+ videos since 2018. His work spans comedy sketches, animation, short films, music, comics, and more.

Ryan's unique blend of humor, artistry, and pop culture knowledge has earned him praise from industry professionals including David Zucker (Airplane!), Rob Schrab (Rick & Morty), and Scott Aukerman (Comedy Bang Bang). His work demonstrates exceptional range—from directing and editing to writing and illustration.

Whether creating comedy content, drawing comics, composing music, or directing short films, Ryan approaches each project as a "Pop Culture Wizard" with complete enthusiasm and dedication to his craft.
